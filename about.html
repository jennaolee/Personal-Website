ABOUT ME

Hey! My name is Jenna. I am a Computing Science student at Simon Fraser University with interests in development, graphics, and AI. Currently, I am working towards an Information Systems concentration to strengthen my foundation in software engineering and database systems. As a Co-op student, I am excited to gain industry experience and apply my knowledge to real-world projects!


When I'm not programming, I enjoy art, music, and exploring the outdoors. I love painting, 3D modelling, animation, and digital illustrations. Recently, I have started crocheting and enjoy making clothing and plush toys. From piano to clarinet to guitar, music has always been a core part of my life. Although I don't play the piano or clarinet often, I love playing songs on my acoustic and electric guitar. With mountains in my backyard, I love snowboarding, hiking, and exploring new trails.
